The guarantee is working — only 2.59% of eligible subs needed a refund
Of 694 eligible subscribers, only 18 (2.59%) successfully completed the 60-day Satisfaction Guarantee requirements and received a refund. The 91.5% "no event" rate means the vast majority of long-term buyers stayed without issue. The elevated CTM-12 rate (6.5%) is expected given it's the largest-volume, highest-commitment plan.
69.5% of refund requests didn't qualify — and weren't refunded
Of 59 refund requests across eligible products, 41 were denied (37 didn't meet requirements / missed the window, 9 escalated to chargebacks). These 37 denied subscribers ran out their subscription terms as normal. The 17 still-active denials represent subscribers who requested but are still being billed — worth monitoring.
4 of 10 chargebacks are still active subscribers
Four CTM-12 Month chargebacks show as "active" — meaning the dispute was filed but the subscription wasn't terminated. This is a high-risk situation: these accounts have disputed a payment yet continue to have access. They should be reviewed and access paused pending resolution.

More products = dramatically longer subscriptions
0 products → 3.53 mo avg. 1 product → 6.41 mo. 2 products → 7.79 mo. 3 products → 10.57 mo. Each additional product adds ~2–3 months of expected retention. These buyers have demonstrated deeper engagement with the MTI ecosystem before committing to a subscription.
PSPro and TTMAX owners have the lowest refund rates
PSPro owners: 0.70% refund/CB rate. TTMAX owners: 0.84%. These are buyers who have invested in premium strategy tools and likely have higher intent and more realistic expectations of the platform. Targeting these product buyers in acquisition should reduce support burden.
UTP + PSPro + TTMAX combo = 11.39 month avg
Subscribers who own UTP, PSPro, AND TTMAX (but not TSPro) average 11.39 months with 0% refund rate across 29 subscribers. The 4-product combo (all four) averages 10.18 months but has a 4% refund rate — slightly elevated, possibly due to higher-expectation buyers. The 3-product combo is the sweet spot.
